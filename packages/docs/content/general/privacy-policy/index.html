Privacy policy

To continuously improve the quality of our services and tailor the content and advertisements to the preferences of our users, we utilize analytical tools such as Google Analytics 4 (GA4), Google Tag Manager (GTM), Meta Pixel, and Microsoft Clarity. These tools aid us in analyzing how users interact with our website, which in turn enables us to optimize its content and functionality. Within Google Analytics, we employ audience segmentation to present personalized ads through Google Ads tools.

We ensure that all data is collected anonymously and is used solely for the purposes of analysis and enhancing the quality of the services offered by our website. We comply with all regulations concerning the protection of personal data and respect privacy policies.
